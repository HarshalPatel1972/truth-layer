# Truth Layer - Chrome Extension

## How to Install and Test

### 1. Load into Chrome
1. Open Google Chrome.
2. Type `chrome://extensions` in the address bar and press Enter.
3. Toggle **Developer mode** on in the top right corner.
4. Click the **Load unpacked** button (top left).
5. Select the folder: 
   `c:\Users\Harshal Patel\Desktop\test-10`

### 2. Open the Side Panel
1. Open any public webpage (e.g., a YouTube video or a news article).
   - *Example:* [Rick Astley on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ) (This enables us to find many Reddit threads!)
2. Look at the top right of your Chrome browser toolbar.
3. You will see a square icon that looks like a **square divided into two columns** (Side Panel icon). It is usually between the "Extensions" (puzzle piece) icon and your Profile picture.
4. Click that **Side Panel** icon.
5. A panel will slide out on the right.
6. Click the dropdown menu at the top of that panel (it might say "Reading List" or "Bookmarks" by default).
7. Select **Truth Layer** from the list.

### 3. See the Results
- The panel will instantly search Reddit for the current URL.
- You should see a list of discussions related to that page.
- Click any card to open the Reddit thread.
